Behind the scenes of the project

Timeproof Cybersoul is a project realized during my studies in the MMi department (Multimedia and Internet professions) of the iut of Belfort-Montbéliard.

My main goal is to apply myself in web design studies; that's why I was very interested in working on this project.

My main goal is to address my future self by sharing one of my current passions: the cyberpunk genre.

The choice of the informational format was made so that my site could also interest others.

I also chose to tackle the theme of cyberpunk because it is related to the future, digital and technology; themes at the heart of modernity although the genre appeared in the 1980s.

Hoping that my site will allow you to discover or cultivate an interest in cyberpunk, I wish you a good visit of my site.

En espérant que mon site vous permette de vous découvrir ou de cultiver un intérêt pour le cyberpunk, je vous souhaite une bonne visite de mon site.

For any contact, please find my information in the footer or on the dedicated page.
