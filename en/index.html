    Very popular in many media, the cyberpunk genre is a mixture of science fiction and dystopia.

    Representing social-political conflicts, it is also known for its highly technological and futuristic universe.

    Having established itself through numerous media and works such as the manga series Gunnm, the movie Akira or more recently the video game Cyberpunk 2077, it remains very popular nowadays. Indeed, it is towards the end of the 1980s that it emerges until its decline in 1990, not without leaving a trace.

    Discover a universe of rebellion and social conflict in a world of neon lights and advanced technology.
